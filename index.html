The Simpsons is a long-running American animated comedy show from Matt Groening.

About The Simpsons

The Simpsons takes place in a town called Springfield and the show centers around and follows the misadventures of the dysfunctional Simpson family: Homer, an neglectful, abusive drunken moron. His overbearing and moralistic wife Marge and their 3 children: Bart, an evil little punk. Lisa, a intelligent and unpopular misfit and Maggie, a cute and adorable baby girl
